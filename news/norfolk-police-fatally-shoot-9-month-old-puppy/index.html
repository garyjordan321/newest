    Norfolk Police Fatally Taser 9-Month-Old Puppy Milo Outside Her Home

    Tragic incident raises questions about police use of force

    By Bron Maher

    Norfolk residents were left shocked and heartbroken after police officers fatally tasered a 9-month-old puppy named Milo on the verge outside her familys home.

    Milo, described by her owners as playful and friendly, was known to enjoy spending time near the edge of her yard, often chasing leaves or playing with her toys.

    The incident occurred during a routine police patrol when officers reportedly stopped near the residence after an alleged call about a loose animal. .

    According to witnesses, Milo was on her familys property, wagging her tail and displaying no signs of aggression when the officers approached.

    Family members and neighbors attempted to intervene, shouting that Milo was a young puppy and entirely harmless.

    Despite these protests, officers deployed a taser, striking the puppy. Milo yelped loudly and fell to the ground, visibly distressed.

    Witnesses reported that Milo struggled for a moment before going still. Her owners rushed to her side but were unable to save her.

    The use of force in this situation has sparked outrage among the Norfolk community, with many questioning whether the officers acted appropriately.

    She was just a baby, Milos owner said tearfully. She wasnt doing anything wrong. She didnt deserve this.

    Animal rights advocates and local residents have condemned the incident, calling for an immediate investigation into the actions of the officers involved.

    Norfolk Police issued a statement expressing regret for the outcome but defended the officers actions, stating that they acted according to their training.

    However, critics argue that training should prioritize de-escalation, particularly when dealing with animals, to prevent unnecessary harm.

    Social media has been flooded with tributes to Milo, with many users sharing pictures of their own pets and demanding justice for the young puppy.

    A candlelight vigil was held outside the familys home, with neighbors and supporters leaving flowers and toys in Milos memory.

    Local officials have promised a thorough review of the incident, with some hinting at possible policy changes to prevent similar tragedies in the future.

    Animal welfare organizations have offered support to Milos family and are calling for mandatory animal behavior training for all police officers.

    The incident has reignited a broader conversation about the use of force by law enforcement and the lack of protocols for handling non-threatening animals.

    Milos family says they are heartbroken but hope her story will lead to meaningful change in how such situations are handled.

    This shouldnt happen to anyone elses pet, Milos owner said. Milo was part of our family, and shell never be forgotten.
